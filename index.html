Our Trading Strategy

A systematic approach to capital allocation and trading that maximizes returns while minimizing risk.

Capital Allocation

Based on a capital of ₹50,00,000, we allocate funds as follows:

  • 60% (₹30,00,000) invested in mutual fund portfolio
  • 30% (₹15,00,000) invested in government securities and bonds
  • 10% (₹5,00,000) held as cash for safety and liquidity

Portfolio Pledging

We pledge the investments to generate additional trading capital:

  • Mutual funds (₹30,00,000) pledged to get ₹24,00,000 trading capital after haircut
  • Government securities (₹15,00,000) pledged to get ₹12,00,000 trading capital after 20% haircut
  • Cash reserve: ₹5,00,000
